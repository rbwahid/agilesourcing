# AgileSourcing User Personas

## Overview

This document outlines the key user personas for the AgileSourcing platform - a generative-AI powered SaaS that transforms fashion design and manufacturing into a data-driven, sustainable process.

| **Location** | Toronto, Canada |
| **Age** | 28-45 |
| **Business Size** | Solo or small team (1-5 people) |
| **Market Size** | 483 designers in Canada, 26,541 in US |

### Goals

- Create collections that resonate with target consumers
- Reduce financial risk of producing unsold inventory
- Build a sustainable, ethical brand identity
- Stay competitive against fast-fashion giants

### Pain Points

- **Uncertainty**: "I spend months designing a collection without knowing if it will sell"

- **Return rates**: High returns eat into already thin margins

### Behaviors

- Active on Instagram and Pinterest for inspiration
- Attends local fashion events and trade shows
- Produces 2-4 collections per year (vs. fast fashion's weekly releases)
- Values quality over quantity
- Environmentally conscious in sourcing decisions

### How AgileSourcing Helps

- Validates designs through real-time social media feedback before production
- Generates AI-powered design variations quickly
- Provides direct access to vetted sustainable suppliers
- Reduces guesswork and financial risk

- **Supplier discovery**: Hard to find reliable sustainable manufacturers
- **Competition**: Competing with online retailers and fast fashion on price
- **Customer expectations**: Shoppers want sustainable options but won't pay premium prices

### Behaviors

- Monitors social media for emerging trends
- Builds relationships with independent designers
- Values personal customer relationships
- Seeks exclusive or limited pieces

### How AgileSourcing Helps

- Access to trending design data to inform purchasing decisions
- Validates which styles will resonate before committing to orders
- Discovers sustainable suppliers for private label opportunities
- Gains competitive intelligence on consumer preferences

- Direct access to North American designers and boutiques
- Lead generation through intelligent matching with buyer needs
- Reduced dependency on expensive trade shows and agents
- Enhanced credibility through platform verification

- Showcases proximity, certifications, and capabilities
- Connects with brands whose designs are already validated (ready to produce)
- Streamlines the quotation and matching process
